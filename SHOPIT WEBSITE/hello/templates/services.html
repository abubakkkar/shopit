Shopping Policy

Order Processing

All orders are processed within 24-48 hours. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been successfully placed.

Shipping & Delivery

  • Standard shipping takes 3-7 business days.
  • Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.
  • Shipping costs are calculated based on weight and destination.

Returns & Refunds

  • Products can be returned within 14 days of delivery.
  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the returned product.
  • Items must be unused and in original packaging.

Customer Support

Our support team is available 24/7 via email and chat. We are committed to resolving all issues quickly and efficiently to ensure a smooth shopping experience.
